Information

Welcome to your ultimate guide to exploring Tokyo, crafted from personal experience to balance the needs of both first-time visitors and seasoned travelers. Whether you’re going for a short three-day trip, a five-day adventure, or an extended stay, this itinerary ensures you’ll experience the best of Tokyo’s blend of tradition and modernity.

When visiting Tokyo, 3 days will suffice to explore the major central parts of the city. But with additional days, you can give yourself time to explore the outskirts of Tokyo that are vastly different from central Tokyo. From serene temples and lush parks to bustling shopping streets and vibrant nightlife, each day is planned to provide a mix of sightseeing, dining, and relaxation. For those familiar with Tokyo, special highlights are included to offer fresh perspectives and hidden gems.

In addition to must-see attractions, practical tips and things to avoid are provided to help you navigate the city like a local. Get ready to embark on an exciting journey through Tokyo, with detailed itineraries and notable highlights for an unforgettable experience.

Enjoy Tokyo!
